About Us

Dog Cove is a dog-group based in Hawke's Bay.
The purpose of this platform is to connect potential, new, and experienced dog owners together.
Through various exchanges forms such as Sharing Photos, Forums, News & Events, and Local Sales.

Our Mission

Hawke's Bay Dog Cove seeks to encourage the growth and care of all canines.
We strive to build and strengthen the community between people and dogs, one community at a time.
